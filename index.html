Industrial MS Product Catalog

High-yield Mild Steel components manufactured for extreme load-bearing and structural stability. We fulfill high-volume, bulk manufacturing runs tailored directly to your engineering blueprints.

MS Foundation Plates

  • Core Material: Premium Mild Steel (IS 2062 Grade)
  • Applications: Civil engineering foundations, heavy machinery base mounting, railway sleeper components, and structural column bases.
  • Capabilities: Precision cutting, sheared edges, custom perforation, and drilling available upon request. Thickness range scaled for heavy industrial apps.

Heavy-Duty MS Washers

  • Core Material: Premium Mild Steel
  • Applications: Scaffolding assembly, railway fastening systems, heavy automotive and industrial hardware.
  • Capabilities: High-volume punching and stamping. Consistent gauge thickness and precise inner/outer diameter ratios for load distribution.

Scaffolding Bottom Cups

  • Core Material: Premium Mild Steel
  • Applications: Crucial foundational component for modular scaffolding systems (Cuplock scaffolding), ensuring a rigid structural connection.
  • Capabilities: Deep-drawn manufacturing, precise cup dimensions for tight-locking mechanisms, and high durability for repeated industrial use.

Industrial Hardware & Shutter Fittings

  • Products Available: L Drop, Bend L Drop, Shutter Kunda (Small, Big, L Kunda, Paan Kunda), Shutter Side Plate, and Tikli.
  • Core Material: Premium Mild Steel
  • Applications: Commercial rolling shutters, heavy-duty industrial doors, and architectural hardware fittings.
  • Capabilities: High-tonnage stamping, anti-corrosion readiness, and standardized designs for bulk hardware wholesalers.

CRC Scrap Bundles

  • Core Material: Cold Rolled Coil (CRC) Steel Scrap
  • Applications: Direct feedstock for induction furnaces, foundry operations, and secondary steel manufacturing meant for remelting purposes.
  • Capabilities: Hydraulically pressed into dense, compact bundles to minimize melt loss, optimize furnace charge density, and reduce energy consumption during the remelting process.

Industrial MS Scrap Trading

  • Trade Category: High-grade Mild Steel Industrial Scrap
  • Sourcing: Byproducts of heavy stamping, precision cutting, and structured demolition.
  • Applications: Direct feedstock for secondary steel smelting, foundries, and heavy recycling plants.
